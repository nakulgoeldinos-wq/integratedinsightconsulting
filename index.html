BRAND EXTENSIONS

Strategic Initiatives, Not Surrogates

What is Brand Extension? A practice where unrestricted goods, products, or services are sold under the same wordmark or logo as prohibited products—creating viable businesses while maintaining brand integrity.

We help create and execute brand extensions for restricted categories—an opportunity to build strategic initiatives rather than be perceived as surrogates. Our approach focuses on enhancing core brand equity through viable business models.

  • Brand Extension Product Mapping , Design, Strategy and Execution.
  • Market Research: Brand Equity Studies, Product Testing, Mystery Shopping
  • Visual Merchandising Audits at retail and for brand programs at HORECA.
  • Brand Extension led Training & Capacity Building Workshops .
  • Regulatory Compliance: CTNR, CBFC, CCPA, ASCI guidelines
